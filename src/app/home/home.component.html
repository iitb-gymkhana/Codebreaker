Eco Ganesha Workshop

The consistent endeavour of NSS IIT Bombay to bring about ecological prosperity with Eco Ganesha, using 100% natural components, to care for the nature that nurtures us. The workshop is intended to motivate use of ecofriendly stuffs in daily life, starting from Mumbai's worshiped Ganesha Idols.

Visit to Vatsalya Trust Orphanage

The volunteers of NSS, IIT Bombay spread smiles in the Vatsalya Trust Orphanage, Kanjurmarg. They conducted an Origami session for the children and also made an attempt to develop their interest in the science stream by demonstrating some scientific experiments.

Biodiversity Mapping

Our volunteers made this small effort to map the trees along the main road and name them! They covered 200+ trees, providing interesting trivia with basic facts. This activity received lot of media attention as well.

Go Cashless

Under the VISAKA program of MHRD, NSS IIT Bombay adopted the Powai Fish Market. We introduced the vendors and consumers to the various modes of cashless transactions and also in installation of UPI apps. We also gave a demo these apps and presented our tutorial videos.
