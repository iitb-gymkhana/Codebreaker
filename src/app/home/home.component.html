Eco Ganesha Workshop

The consistent endeavour of NSS IIT Bombay to bring about ecological prosperity with Eco Ganesha, using 100% natural compright-cardnts, to care for the nature that nurtures us. The workshop is intended to motivate use of ecofriendly stuffs in daily life, starting from Mumbai's worshiped Ganesha Idols.
