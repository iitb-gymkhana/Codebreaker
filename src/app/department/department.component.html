Green Campus
Green Campus handles the genre of preserving and rejuvenating flora and fauna. We focus on the areas inside institute while some of the recent initiatives are also intended for non-institute people. We have our full-fledged nursery inside the nursery and all aspects of gardening are introduced to Green Campus volunteers in the nursery. We make conscious efforts to increase the bio-diversity in the campus, by making attempts to plant seeds of plants found in different parts of India in our nursery. Below is the list of the activities we do. We feel the ecstasy of being in close relation with nature.

Collection of Saplings
We collect naturally grown saplings in the institute and grow them in our Nursery in plastic covers till they are self dependant to grow in land.
Transplantation
We identify the areas which lack green cover inside institute and transplant the tree saplings over there. We have had plantations in the area around hostel-15 recently, which was severely lacking green cover due to multiple construction activities.
